## oh the shamelessness
This is an unattributed blog for documenting demonstrations, experiments, and personal explorations. Posts are intentionally uncredited focusing on content over authorship and cover topics I’m asked to showcase or find intriguing. Built for simplicity, speed, and privacy.

### What This Is
A public notebook of technical demonstrations, solved problems, and exploratory thinking. Content is intentionally unattributed to:  
- **Reduce bias** (yours and mine)  
- **Encourage reuse** without attribution pressure  
- **Prioritize information** over identity

### Principles  

1. **No authorship**:  
   - Posts stand on their own merit.  
   - No bylines, bios, or social links.  
   - Authority comes from clarity—not credentials.  

2. **No gatekeeping**:  
   - Assume readers are competent but unfamiliar.  
   - Explain acronyms, link to fundamentals, but avoid "beginner-friendly" condescension.  
   - No "obvious" dismissals; document the invisible steps.  

3. **No permanence**:  
   - Content may evolve or vanish without notice.  
   - Corrections overwrite; no changelogs or apologies for past errors.  
   - Dead links stay dead. The internet is ephemeral by design.  

4. **No ownership**:  
   - Use, remix, or discard anything here—no credit needed.  
   - No licenses, no disclaimers, no "terms." (Default: [CC0](https://creativecommons.org/publicdomain/zero/1.0/))  

5. **No performance**:  
   - No analytics, no "most-read" lists, no SEO games.  
   - Words exist to be read—not to be counted.  
   
  
### Why These Rules?  
- **Antidote to influencer culture**: Remove the author, and the idea gets tested, not the persona.  
- **Anti-hoarding**: Knowledge decays; this site embraces its own expiration date.  
- **Focus on utility**: If a post can’t survive without its author’s reputation, it wasn’t clear enough.
